###  GeeGee Phones Stock Analysis 

#### Project Overview

This project analyzes **GeeGee Phones stock distribution** across retailers, warehouses, stores, sales areas, product models, and colors.
The goal is to provide actionable insights that help management understand stock availability, demand concentration, and distribution efficiency.

The dashboard focuses on **Y04 and Y21d phone models** and highlights key stock trends using interactive visualizations.

---

#### Objectives

- Identify products with the highest stock availability
- Analyze stock distribution across retailers, warehouses, and stores
- Highlight top sales areas and regions with the highest inventory
- Compare stock levels by product model and color
- Support inventory planning and decision-making

---

#### Dashboard Insights

  -  **Key Highlights**

- Highest Product Model in Stock: **Y04 (+64)**
- Most Available Product Color: **Green**
- Store with the Highest Stock: **HQ 8664**

---

#### Visual Analysis Breakdown

  -  **Number of Stocks per Retailer**

   - Compares stock levels of **Y04 and Y21d models** across multiple retailers
   - Helps identify retailers with **overstock or low inventory**

 -   **Top 10 Sales Areas with Highest Stock**

   - Reveals **regions and states** holding the most stock
   - Useful for aligning stock levels with **sales demand**

-  **Number of Stocks per Warehouse**

   - Highlights warehouses with **high and low stock volumes**
   - Supports better **warehouse utilization and redistribution**

- **Top 10 Stores with Highest Number of Retailers**

   - Shows stores with the **widest retail reach**
   - Indicates strong distribution hubs

- **Top 10 Stores with the Highest Number of Stocks**

   - Identifies stores holding **bulk inventory**
   - Useful for risk management and stock balancing

- **Available Product Colors (Y04 & Y21d)**

   - Compares availability of **Gold, Red, and Green** variants
   - Green emerges as the **most stocked color**
